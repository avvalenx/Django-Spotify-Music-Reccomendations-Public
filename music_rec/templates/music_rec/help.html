Spotify Music Discovery

1. Signup

Click the button to signup and create an account. After you create an account you will be prompted to sign into your spotify account to link it with your website account.

2. Enter up to 5 songs, 5 artists, and 5 genres

Click Get Music Recommendations to enter up to 5 songs, 5 artists, and 5 genres that you want to base the music recommendations on. You can enter as many or as few as you want and when you are ready click continue.

After clicking on songs or artists you can search for any song or artist you want. Click on the song or artists you want and click the add to list button to add them to the list of items to get recommendations on

After clicking on genres click up to 5 genres to add to the list of items to get recommendations on.

3. Enter parameters or continue without them

Adjust the sliders and click continue or click continue without parameters to continue without having the sliders effect the song recommendations you choose. Enter a number between 1 - 100 songs to be reccomended

4. Name the Playlist and add it to your Spotify account

You can see the the songs and artists Spotify has suggested based on your inputs and can now name the playlist and add it to your Spotify account.
